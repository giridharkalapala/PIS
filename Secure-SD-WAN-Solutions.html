Network Modernization

Secure SD-WAN Solutions

Secure SD-WAN (Software-Defined Wide Area Network) is transforming enterprise connectivity by intelligently managing traffic across multiple network links to ensure high performance, reliability, and security for distributed locations.

At Progresso Infosystems, we help organizations deploy SD-WAN architectures that integrate encryption, next-gen firewall protection, and real-time threat intelligence—delivering a unified, secure, and scalable networking experience.

With the growing adoption of cloud applications and remote work models, our Secure SD-WAN solutions empower businesses to achieve superior performance, optimized bandwidth usage, and end-to-end data protection—all while reducing operational costs and complexity.

Advanced Secure SD-WAN Solutions

Enhanced Security

Integrated encryption, next-gen firewalls, and real-time threat intelligence safeguard every connection across the network, ensuring end-to-end protection for all users and data.

Improved Performance

Smart traffic routing selects the most efficient network paths to minimize latency, optimize bandwidth, and deliver superior performance across all connected sites.

Cost Efficiency

Eliminate the dependency on costly MPLS circuits by leveraging broadband and LTE links—reducing operational expenses while maintaining secure, high-speed connectivity.

Scalable Flexibility

Easily expand your network to support new sites, users, and applications—ensuring consistent, secure performance across growing and complex environments.

Unlock the Power of Hybrid Cloud and Secure SD-WAN

Progresso Infosystems combines the flexibility of hybrid cloud with the intelligence of SD-WAN to deliver a robust, secure, and scalable digital foundation. Our architecture optimizes performance, reduces operational costs, and ensures complete control over your distributed network environments.

Centralized Control

Gain real-time visibility and control of your entire SD-WAN network through a unified dashboard — enabling simplified management, performance monitoring, and proactive troubleshooting.

Zero Trust Security

Built on Zero Trust principles, our solutions continuously verify users, devices, and connections to prevent unauthorized access — ensuring uncompromised data protection.

Automated Failover

Maintain uninterrupted connectivity with intelligent traffic routing that automatically switches to backup links — ensuring continuous uptime and business resilience.

Intelligent Threat Prevention

Leverage AI-driven analytics and real-time detection to identify and neutralize cyber threats — safeguarding critical network infrastructure from evolving attacks.
