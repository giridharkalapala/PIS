Cybersecurity Awareness

A Growing Threat to Organizations Worldwide

Phishing continues to be one of the most deceptive cyber threats, exploiting human trust instead of technical flaws. Attackers impersonate legitimate brands through emails, texts, or fake websites to steal credentials and financial information.

Studies reveal that human error drives 95% of data breaches, with phishing responsible for nearly one in six incidents. Beyond financial loss, such attacks severely impact brand reputation, customer confidence, and operational continuity.

To counter this growing risk, organizations must move beyond reactive measures. Employee awareness, phishing simulations, and continuous training help build a proactive defense that stops attacks before they succeed.
